# KPI Dashboard One-Pager (Template)

## Weekly
- Leads / SQLs / Opps created
- Conversion rates by stage
- CPL by channel
- Sales-cycle aging (days in stage)

## Monthly
- ARR / MRR, Net New ARR, churned ARR
- CAC (lag-adjusted), Payback, LTV:CAC
- Retention (GRR/NRR or churn %)
- Pipeline coverage (next 90 days)

## Quarterly
- ICP fit by segment (win + retention)
- Channel concentration risk
- Pricing/packaging iteration outcomes
- Hiring stage-gates met (yes/no)
